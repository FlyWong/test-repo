Technology is an integral part of Singapore’s education system.

Entering the Mobile Age, technology is slowly but surely transforming the Singapore’s private education.

Free learning sites like Khan Academy and, more relevant to Singapore’s GCE syllabus, Open Lecture, have allowed students to learn any topic, anywhere and anytime as long as they have a laptop / smart device and internet connection.  

Students can have their questions clarified with EduSnap, the mobile app that enables students to upload their questions for volunteer tutors to solve for Free!

Good tuition though, is more than delivering content and providing solutions.
